# Volunteer Hours Tracking

Track and report volunteer contributions to demonstrate community support.

## Why Track Hours?

**For Funders:**
- Demonstrate community buy-in
- Calculate in-kind contributions
- Show volunteer value ($28.54/hour national value)

**For Volunteers:**
- Recognition and appreciation
- Service verification letters
- Milestone awards

**For Tax Purposes:**
- Volunteer time = in-kind donation
- Include in Form 990
- Show community support %

## Best Practices

### Accurate Tracking
1. Log hours within 48 hours of service
2. Include prep and travel time
3. Separate direct service vs admin
4. Get supervisor approval

### Regular Reporting
- Weekly volunteer hour updates
- Monthly summary to leadership
- Quarterly board reports
- Annual impact calculation
