## Grant Application Process

### 1. Prospect Research
- Identify aligned funders
- Review eligibility criteria
- Check funding priorities
- Note application deadlines

### 2. Letter of Inquiry (LOI)
- Brief 2-3 page overview
- Submit before full proposal
- Get funder feedback
- Saves time if not aligned

### 3. Full Proposal
**Required Components:**
- Executive summary
- Need statement
- Program description
- Budget and timeline
- Evaluation plan
- Organization background
- Board list and financials

### 4. Stewardship & Reporting
- Thank you letter within 48 hours
- Regular progress updates
- Interim and final reports
- Impact documentation
- Relationship building for renewal

## Grant Types

### Foundation Grants
- **Private foundations** - Family foundations, community foundations
- **Corporate foundations** - Company giving programs
- **Average award:** $10K-$50K
- **Typical term:** 1 year

### Government Grants
- **Federal** - Dept of Education, HHS, SAMHSA
- **State** - Arizona DCS, education grants
- **County/City** - Local youth services
- **Average award:** $50K-$250K
- **Typical term:** 1-3 years

### Program-Related Grants
- Education & tutoring
- Life skills development
- Housing & transition services
- Scholarship funds

## Grant Writing Tips

### Strong Proposals Include:
1. **Compelling need** - Data + stories
2. **Clear goals** - Specific, measurable outcomes
3. **Proven approach** - Evidence-based methods
4. **Realistic budget** - Detailed and justified
5. **Evaluation plan** - How you'll measure success
6. **Sustainability** - How program continues after grant

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:
- Missing deadlines
- Not following guidelines
- Weak outcome metrics
- Unrealistic budgets
- Generic boilerplate
- No funder research

## Funder Relationship Tips

### After Award:
- Send thank you within 48 hours
- Invite to site visit
- Include in newsletter/events
- Submit reports early
- Apply for renewal 6 months before end

### After Rejection:
- Request feedback
- Thank for consideration
- Maintain relationship
- Apply again if appropriate
