## Campaign Timeline Best Practices

**8 weeks before:**
- Set goal and case for support
- Identify lead donors
- Create communication plan

**6 weeks before:**
- Secure lead gifts (25-30% of goal)
- Prepare marketing materials
- Train volunteer solicitors

**4 weeks before:**
- Launch campaign publicly
- Email/mail appeals
- Social media campaign

**2 weeks before:**
- Mid-campaign push
- Thank early donors
- Follow up on pending asks

**Final week:**
- Last-chance messaging
- Phone calls to prospects
- Social media countdown

**After close:**
- Thank all donors within 48 hours
- Impact report within 30 days
- Donor retention strategy

## Tips for Success

1. **Start with lead gifts** - Secure 30% of goal before public launch
2. **Matching gifts** - Double donor impact, increase participation
3. **Impact stories** - Show donors where their money goes
4. **Multi-channel** - Email, mail, phone, social media
5. **Urgency** - Deadline creates action
