# People Overview

The People P focuses on your most important asset: your team. Manage consultant capacity, track utilization, and optimize workforce allocation.

## Key Metrics

The dashboard shows 4 critical workforce metrics:
- **Total Team Members** - Current headcount
- **Avg Utilization** - Team capacity usage percentage
- **Active Engagements** - Client projects with team assigned
- **Team Satisfaction** - Internal NPS score

## Sub-Pages

### Team Members
View all consultants with detailed profiles, specializations, and current utilization. Filter by department and role.

### Capacity Planning
Plan resource allocation across engagements. Identify over-utilized and under-utilized team members.

### Onboarding
Track new client onboarding progress through the 5-milestone process.

## Quick Actions

- Click **View** on any metric card to drill into details
- Use footer navigation to switch between P's
- Press **Cmd+K** for quick page search

## Best Practices

- Monitor utilization weekly to prevent burnout (>90% = red flag)
- Balance senior/junior consultant ratios on engagements
- Track onboarding velocity to optimize client ramp-up
