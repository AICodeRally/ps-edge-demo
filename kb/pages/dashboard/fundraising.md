# Donor Management

Manage your donor database and track giving patterns.

## Current Donor Base

- **2,847 total donors**
- **87% retention rate** (national avg: 43%)
- **$1.2M raised this year**
- **42 major donors** ($5,000+ lifetime)

## Key Features

### Donor Segmentation
- **Individual donors** - Personal relationships, appeals
- **Corporate sponsors** - Partnership opportunities
- **Foundations** - Grant relationships
- **Monthly sustainers** - Recurring revenue

### Donor Health Indicators
- **Active** - Gave in last 12 months
- **Lapsed** - No gift in 12-24 months (re-engage)
- **New** - First-time donors (stewardship critical)

## AI-Powered Insights

Ask **AskNP** about:
- "Which donors haven't given recently?"
- "Show me major donor prospects"
- "What's our average gift size?"

**OpsChief** can alert you to:
- Lapsed major donors needing outreach
- Donor retention trends
- Campaign performance issues

## Best Practices

### Donor Stewardship
1. **Thank within 48 hours** of gift
2. **Personal touch** for gifts $1,000+
3. **Impact updates** quarterly for major donors
4. **Recognition** in annual report

### Monthly Donor Program
- 3x lifetime value vs one-time donors
- 92% retention rate
- Predictable revenue stream
