# Events Management

Plan and execute events for fundraising, community engagement, and beneficiary services.

## Event Types

### Fundraising Events
- **Annual Gala** - Signature event, $50K+ goal
- **Golf Tournament** - Corporate sponsors
- **5K Run/Walk** - Community participation
- **Online Auctions** - Reach broader audience

### Community Engagement
- **Foster Care Awareness Month** (May)
- **Back-to-School Drive** (August)
- **Holiday Gift Program** (December)
- **Community Service Days**

### Program Events
- **Youth recognition ceremonies**
- **Scholarship award dinners**
- **Volunteer appreciation**
- **Program graduations**

## Event Planning Timeline

### 6 Months Before
- Set date and venue
- Form planning committee
- Create budget
- Secure sponsors

### 3 Months Before
- Finalize program/agenda
- Begin marketing
- Open registration
- Recruit volunteers

### 1 Month Before
- Confirm all details
- Send reminders
- Finalize attendance count
- Prepare materials

### Week Of
- Final walk-through
- Volunteer briefing
- Setup and logistics
- Day-of coordination

### After Event
- Thank sponsors and attendees
- Financial reconciliation
- Impact report
- Debrief and lessons learned

## Key Metrics

**Track for Each Event:**
- **Registration** - vs capacity
- **Attendance** - actual vs registered
- **Revenue** - vs goal
- **Expenses** - vs budget
- **Net income** - profit/loss
- **Donor acquisition** - new donors gained

## Event Success Tips

### Fundraising Events
1. **Secure sponsors early** - Covers costs, ensures profit
2. **Attractive packages** - Tiered sponsorship levels
3. **Personal asks** - Board and committee outreach
4. **Compelling program** - Entertainment, speakers, impact stories
5. **Auction strategy** - Silent and live items

### Community Events
1. **Accessibility** - Free or low cost
2. **Family-friendly** - Activities for all ages
3. **Partner organizations** - Co-host for broader reach
4. **Media coverage** - Raise awareness
5. **Volunteer roles** - Engage supporters
