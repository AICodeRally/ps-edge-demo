## Form 990 Checklist

**IRS Form 990 - Due May 15, 2025**

AFFCF will file **Form 990** (full form) based on:
- Fiscal year: January 1 - December 31, 2024
- Gross receipts: ~$2.8M
- Total assets: >$500K

### Required Information

**Part I - Summary:**
- Mission statement
- Program service accomplishments
- Revenue and expense totals

**Part III - Program Services:**
- Describe each major program
- Expenses allocated by program
- Beneficiaries served
- Measurable outcomes

**Part VI - Governance:**
- Board composition and meetings
- Conflict of interest policy

## Best Practices

### Document Everything
- Board meeting minutes
- Financial statements
- Policies and procedures
- Program evaluations
- Grant agreements

### Calendar Management
- Add all deadlines to compliance calendar
- Set reminders 60, 30, and 7 days before
- Assign responsible parties
- Track completion status

### Quality Control
- Have multiple reviewers
- Allow time for revisions
- Keep prior year copies for reference
- Document submission confirmation
