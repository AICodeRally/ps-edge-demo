# Team Members

View and manage all consultants in your organization. Track specializations, current utilization, and capacity.

## Stat Cards Explained

- **Total Consultants** - All team members in the system
- **Avg Utilization** - Overall capacity usage (target: 75-85%)
- **Weekly Capacity** - Total billable hours available
- **Over Capacity** - Consultants at 90%+ utilization (risk of burnout)
- **Under-Utilized** - Consultants below 50% (available for new work)

## Table Columns

- **Name & Title** - Consultant identifier and role
- **Department** - Organizational division
- **Role** - SENIOR_CONSULTANT, CONSULTANT, JUNIOR_CONSULTANT
- **Specializations** - Areas of expertise (max 2 shown)
- **Utilization** - Current capacity usage with visual bar
- **Capacity** - Weekly billable hours available
- **Rate** - Default billing rate per hour

## Actions

- **Filter by Department** - Use dropdown to narrow view
- **Click any row** - View consultant details
- **+ Add Team Member** - Onboard new consultant

## Best Practices

- Keep senior consultants at 80-85% utilization (allow time for mentoring)
- Balance junior consultants at 70-75% (learning curve)
- Flag anyone over 90% for immediate reallocation
- Review weekly to prevent resource conflicts
