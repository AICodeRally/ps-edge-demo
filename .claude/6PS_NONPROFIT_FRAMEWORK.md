## Separation of Concerns

### PIPELINE vs PROFIT
**PIPELINE = Future dollars**
- Proposals not yet won
- Sales forecasting
- Revenue projections
- Deal probability weighting

**PROFIT = Actual dollars**
- Invoices issued
- Revenue recognized
- Cash collected
- Margins realized

**Example:**
- $500K campaign engagement in PIPELINE â†’ Proposal stage (weighted 40% = $200K forecast)
- Same engagement in PROFIT (after contract signed) â†’ $500K revenue recognized over 12 months

### PROCESS vs PRACTICE
**PROCESS = HOW (delivery execution)**
- Running active engagements
- Tracking deliverables
- Managing client communication
- Quality assurance

**PRACTICE = WHAT (service offerings)**
- 11 service lines we sell
- Methodologies we use
- Training we provide
- IP we develop

### PURPOSE vs PERFORMANCE
Both measure outcomes, but different lenses:

**PURPOSE = Mission outcomes**
- Nonprofit sector impact
- Client mission advancement
- Social good created

**PERFORMANCE = Business outcomes**  
- Operational KPIs
- Efficiency metrics
- Financial results
